Support the Project

CortexJS is an open source project under the MIT license. The development and maintenance of the project represents a substantial effort. One-time donors and recurring sponsors contribute to the ongoing development effort of the project.

We welcome both individual and corporate donors and sponsors. If you are a commercial entity, encourage the business partners in your organization to provide financial support to open source projects.

We accept donations via PayPal.

We can also consider short-term development contracts for specific features or maintenance of the project. Get in touch!

If you are not ready to make a financial contribution, starring the project in GitHub (here or here), promoting the project on social media or sending a quick thank you is always very much appreciated.
